Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

🍽️ Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We offer vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Please inform your server of any allergies or dietary needs, and our kitchen will take every precaution to accommodate you safely.

👶 Do you have a kids' menu?

Yes! We offer a special menu for children 12 and under, featuring smaller portions of our favorite dishes at family-friendly prices.

🐕 Are dogs allowed on the patio?

Well-behaved dogs are welcome on our outdoor patio. We even provide water bowls and occasional treats! Service animals are welcome throughout the restaurant.

🎂 Can you accommodate large parties?

Yes! For parties of 9 or more, please call us directly at (555) 123-4567. We also offer private dining options for special celebrations and corporate events.

🎁 Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, gift cards are available for purchase in any amount at the restaurant or online. They're the perfect gift for any occasion!

📱 Is WiFi available?

Yes, we offer complimentary high-speed WiFi for all our guests. Ask your server for the password, or find it printed on your receipt.
